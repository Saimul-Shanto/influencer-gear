G.M.B Akash

Bangladeshi Photographer GMB Akash's passion for photography began in 1996. As a young man he became fascinated with the interpersonal relationships he was able to develop with his subjects through the lens of his camera. Growing up in a developing country filled with millions of impoverished people and abused children, GMB Akash had access to the darkest corner of the world for which he was passionate. But at some point he realized that just effectively reporting the human rights injustices through his images was not enough. He wanted to find ways to help the subjects of abuse and suffering in his photographs as well.
